Exquisite Corpse...


...Is a collaborative word or picture game that was originally invented by Surrealists in the early twentieth century. The first player writes a word or sentence, then the next player builds on that sentence, and so on. The catch: a player can only see the sentence or word that the previous player wrote. Try it out!

How to play
  • Enter a sentence and hit submit.
  • Play until you've reached the desired number of lines.
  • Click on "Show the Corpse" to reveal the story.
  • Click the clear button to start over.

A little game utilizing Javascript, jQuery and Skeleton by Franz Knupfer. To see more of his code, check out github.
